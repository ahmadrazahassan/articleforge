TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS:
===========================================

1. DESCRIPTIONS: More detailed descriptions = better articles
   - Good: "Technology reviews"
   - Better: "Comprehensive technology product reviews and comparisons for consumers"

2. FORMAT: Any format works! The parser is smart
   - CSV with commas
   - Tab-separated (Excel copy-paste)
   - Separators: -, :, |
   - JSON arrays
   - Simple lists

3. QUANTITY: Paste 1-200 items at once
   - Small batch: 5-10 items
   - Medium batch: 20-50 items
   - Large batch: 100-200 items

4. VALIDATION: Parser shows which items are valid
   - Minimum name length: 2 characters
   - Minimum description: 10 characters
   - Invalid items are filtered out automatically

5. SETTINGS: Apply same settings to all items
   - Article Type: Review, Guide, Tutorial, etc.
   - Length: Short, Medium, Long, Extra Long
   - Tone: Professional, Friendly, Technical, etc.
   - Language: English, Spanish, French, etc.
