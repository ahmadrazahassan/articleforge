## üéØ Usage Guidelines

### Do's ‚úÖ
- Use #d1f300 for primary CTAs
- Use #a8e765 for success states
- Use #4892db for information
- Use #137a3c for text and borders
- Use #fff8e8 for backgrounds (solid, no gradients)
- Maintain high contrast for accessibility
- Use opacity for subtle variations
- Keep color usage consistent

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- Don't use gradients
- Don't mix with old color scheme
- Don't use low contrast combinations
- Don't forget accessibility standards
- Don't overuse lime accent
- Don't use pure black or white

## ‚ôø Accessibility
